Bio:

Hello! My name is Milo Bougetz-Aulbach; I'm a UC San Diego graduate with a bachelor's of science in Mathematics and Computer Science and a minor in Music, passionate about harnessing the power of technology to solve complex problems.

My primary interests lie in web and application development, quantitative finance, machine learning, and programming for audio software such as VST plugins. My strong mathematical foundation, with a deep understanding of graph theory and algorithm analysis and optimization, significantly enhances my problem-solving skills and contributes to my effectiveness as a developer.

Alongside my academic background, I have some hobbyist experience in developing third-party video game modifications, reflecting my enthusiasm for programming and creativity.

Outside of tech, I'm an avid reader and aspiring writer of speculative fiction, driven by a love for imaginative storytelling. I'm also a composer, guitarist, and music producer, creating my own unique blend of metal, orchestral, and electronic music.

I'm excited to bring my diverse skills and passions to a software engineering role, where I can contribute to innovative projects and continue learning and growing in the tech industry.
